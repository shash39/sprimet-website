About Us

Founded in the late 1950s by Shri Vidya Prakash as Tencom Industrial Corporation, we were one of the first to import spring manufacturing technology from Germany and the Soviet Union. Today, as Sprimet Springs, we continue this legacy with a focus on advanced, high-performance spring solutions.

We have served clients such as Indian Railways, Ordnance Factory Jabalpur, Bosch, Kalyani Brakes, and Hitachi Astemo, offering full-service design, development, and manufacturing of spring systems.
